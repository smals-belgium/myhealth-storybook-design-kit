# Color (Legacy)

> Colors are designed to communicate actions, state and direction within the interface.
> Blue and green are My Health's main colors, supplemented with black for extra oomph.
> In addition, there are sub-colors for a more personal and warmer palette, which fits in well with the core values.

## Color tokens

Our color palette is recored as global tokens. These can be directly used, and are inherited by all other token types.

### Primary brand colors

Primary brand colors are the main and most important colors. They are the colors of the logo, and therefore the recognizable color of MyHealth.
